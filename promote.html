🎓 About Students of Canada

Students of Canada is a mobile-first platform helping students discover part-time jobs through trusted peer referrals. With 70% of jobs in Canada filled via networking, we're building the first real student work graph — showing students where their peers work and how to get in.

We're on a mission to level the playing field for student employment.
